Astral Plane Terrain Cell State Model v1

Canonical terminology (frozen law):
- cell: place truth (a coordinate slot in world space).
- layer: z-index slice of cells.
- block: structural denomination occupying one cell.
- microblock: one of 27 internal block units.
- measure: smallest economic matter unit (equal to one microblock).
- pile: loose matter aggregation over one or more cells.
- volume: total occupied 3D extent measured in cells.
- contained_matter: matter held inside a container form.
- refined_matter: transformed matter preserving measured quantity.
- assimilated_matter: substrate identity retained under assimilation law.
- reserved_matter: matter marked non-consumable for claimed flows.
- tainted_matter: matter flagged unsafe/restricted for normal flows.

Cell schema:
- coord: (x,y,z)
- occupied: true/false
- state: full_block | loose_matter | assimilated_matter | contained_matter
- measures: 0..27
- temperature_c: baseline placeholder
