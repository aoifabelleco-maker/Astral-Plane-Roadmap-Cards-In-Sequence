Loose Matter Stack Split/Merge Truth Law v1

- Mining outputs loose stacks with explicit truth fields, not anonymous blobs.
- Stack quantity compresses measures only; it does not erase ownership/legality/risk truth.

Hard merge blockers:
- legality state mismatch
- ownership/claim mismatch
- contamination family mismatch
- curse/omen mismatch
- freshness band mismatch

Split law:
- child stack inherits legality, ownership, contamination family, omen, freshness, and warning context.

Legal merge law:
- measures are added.
- warning chips are union-preserved by strongest severity.
- inspect text must retain actionable warning context.
