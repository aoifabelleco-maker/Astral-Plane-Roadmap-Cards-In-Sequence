Astral Plane Terrain Occupation Rules v1

- occupied = true when measures > 0.
- occupied = false when measures = 0.
- full_block requires occupied=true and measures=27.
- loose_matter requires occupied=true and measures in [1..26].
- assimilated_matter requires occupied=true and measures in [1..27].
- contained_matter requires occupied=true and measures in [1..27].
- reserved_matter / tainted_matter are policy tags layered over the base form.
- temperature_c baseline placeholder defaults to 12.0 C.
