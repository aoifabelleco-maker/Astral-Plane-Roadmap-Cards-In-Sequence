Astral Plane Terrain Block/Form Law v1

Denomination law:
- 1 block = 27 measures.
- measure equality does not guarantee use equality.

Form distinctions (must not be conflated):
- walkable_floor: locomotion-supporting traversable form.
- structural_block: solid support block form.
- loose_matter: non-structural dispersed matter form.
- contained_matter: matter in a container boundary.

State notes:
- A full structural block occupies exactly one cell and has 27 measures.
- Decomposed matter may spread as loose form while conserving total measures.
- Assimilated/refined/reserved/tainted flags modify policy, not denomination.
