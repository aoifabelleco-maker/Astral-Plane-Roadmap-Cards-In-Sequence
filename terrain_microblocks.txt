Astral Plane Terrain Microblock Decomposition v1

- Internal decomposition uses 27 microblocks per block (3x3x3).
- 1 microblock = 1 measure.
- full_block => measures = 27
- loose_matter => measures = 1..26
- assimilated_matter => measures = 1..27
- contained_matter => measures = 1..27 (container-bounded)
- free cell => measures = 0
