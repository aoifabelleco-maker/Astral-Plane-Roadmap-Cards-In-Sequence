Assimilated/Reserved/Feed State Truth Law v1

- Assimilated matter is not ordinary loose trade matter.
- Reserved matter remains intentionally withheld for planned use (build, portal, ritual, crisis stock).
- Feed-eligible matter may enter in-process feed.
- In-process feed may produce assimilated growth or rejected/diverted residue.
- Truth and assimilation summaries must report these states separately.
