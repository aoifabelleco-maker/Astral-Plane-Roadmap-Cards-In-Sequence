Core Assimilation Law v1

- Returned foreign matter auto-routes through home core on successful return.
- Assimilation converts feed-approved matter into owned plane growth substrate.
- Assimilated matter is no longer ordinary loose economy matter.
- Reserved matter remains intentionally withheld for future planned use.

Matter-state boundary:
- loose_matter: tradable/buildable economy matter.
- reserved_matter: intentionally withheld stock.
- feed_eligible_matter: candidate input for core feed routing.
- in_process_feed: matter currently in assimilation pipeline.
- assimilated_matter: surrendered to realm growth.
- rejected_or_diverted_residue: non-assimilated output.
